My Information

Hello! My name is Pinpada Kanokwuttipong, and I’m 13 years old. I live in the vibrant city of Bangkok, Thailand, where I’m currently studying as a secondary school student. Life as a student can be busy, but it’s also full of fun moments and new things to learn every day.
Unlike many people my age, I’m not a big fan of reading books or drawing — those just aren’t my favorite activities. Even so, I always try to express myself in other creative ways. People often describe me as someone who is very creative, and maybe even kind, which I take as a big compliment.
One of the most interesting things about me is my huge LEGO collection. I’ve collected over 100,000 LEGO pieces from different sets, and I love building and creating new things from them. It’s a hobby that really lets my imagination run wild, and I never get bored of it!
In the future, one of my biggest dreams is to travel the world and become a diplomat. I want to explore new cultures, meet people from different countries, and represent my country in a positive and respectful way. It’s a job that requires good communication, understanding, and kindness — qualities I hope to keep building as I grow up.
